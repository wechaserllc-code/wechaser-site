制造企业的知识沉淀在三个地方：老工程师的脑子里、塞满文件的硬盘、抄来抄去的微信群。

当 AI 推理成本进入工业级可承受区间，这些知识第一次有可能被真正调用——不是搜索引擎式检索，而是带上下文、带工艺逻辑、带授权边界的回答。

WeChaser 只做一件事：把这一刻的可能性，落到你的车间、销售、客服三条主线上。

Manufacturing knowledge lives in three unreachable places: the heads of senior engineers, hard drives full of static documents, and chat groups nobody can search.

As inference cost finally drops into the industrial zone, this knowledge becomes addressable — not as keyword search, but as answers grounded in process logic, with permission boundaries intact.

WeChaser does one thing: land that possibility onto your shop floor, sales desk, and support line.
