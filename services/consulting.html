01 / 是给谁的Who it's for

不是所有公司
都该上 AI。 Not every company
should adopt AI.

✓ 适合的公司✓ Good fit

  • 年营收 5000 万 - 5 亿，能内部决策的中型企业
  • Mid-market: ¥50M-¥500M revenue, with internal decision authority
  • 有 3 年以上业务沉淀（文档 / 客户档案 / 工艺记录）
  • 3+ years of accumulated knowledge (docs / customer files / process records)
  • 核心业务环节有明显效率瓶颈或经验外流风险
  • Identifiable bottleneck — or knowledge-loss risk — in a core workflow
  • 决策人愿意花时间过 1-2 次深度访谈
  • A decision-maker willing to invest 1-2 deep interview sessions

✗ 不合适的✗ Not a fit

  • "想搞个 AI 聊天框"作为唯一目标
  • Goal is "we want a chatbot" with no further depth
  • 预算少于 10 万 — 试点都跑不起来
  • Budget under ~¥100K — can't even fund a real pilot
  • 没有真实业务数据，只想拿 AI 做营销卖点
  • No real internal data — AI is just a marketing line
  • 期望 1-2 周看到 ROI
  • Expect ROI within 1-2 weeks

02 / ROADMAP

AI 转型路线图AI transformation roadmap

Phased: what to ship at 3 / 6 / 12 months. Each phase tied to verifiable outputs.

One business line + one team + 8-12 weeks, with success metrics and a kill criterion.

试点 SOWPilot SOW
04 / STACK

技术选型矩阵Tech-selection matrix

开源 vs 商用 vs 自研三轴对比，给出符合企业合规要求的推荐组合。

Open-source vs commercial vs in-house — three-axis comparison with a compliance-aware recommended bundle.

对比表 + 评分Matrix + scores
05 / TEAM

人才配置建议Team configuration

06 / RISK

Data security, compliance, model interpretability, org friction — surfaced upfront with mitigation plans.

风险矩阵 + 预案Risk matrix + playbook
